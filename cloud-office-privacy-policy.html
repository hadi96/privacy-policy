Privacy Policy

Effective Date : January 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled by the Cloud Office mobile application (the "Application"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding our data handling practices.

1. Overview

The Application is an independent browser utility designed to facilitate access to Microsoft Office 365 web services. It functions as a specialized interface that renders official Microsoft web endpoints within a secure environment.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data.

The Application does not have a backend server and does not transmit any user information to us or any third party other than Microsoft’s official servers.

  • Authentication: All login procedures are performed directly on Microsoft’s official authentication servers. The Application does not see, intercept, or store your credentials.
  • Documents: Your files are stored directly on your personal or organization’s OneDrive/SharePoint storage. We have no access to your data.
  • Analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking software.

3. Use of Permissions

To provide a functional Office experience, the Application may request the following permissions:

  • Camera & Microphone: Used exclusively for features such as Microsoft Teams meetings or voice-to-text. This data is never recorded or stored by the Application.
  • Storage: Used solely to allow downloading documents to your device or uploading local files to your cloud storage.
  • Internet Access: Required to connect to Microsoft Office 365 services.

By using this Application, you are also subject to Microsoft’s Privacy Statement.

The Application may display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may use device identifiers to serve ads. View Google’s Privacy Policy for more details.
