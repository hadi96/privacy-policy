1. Information We Collect

1.1. Account Information

When you register for an account, we collect the following information:

  • Email Address: Required for account creation, login, and communication purposes.
  • Password: Securely encrypted and hashed by Firebase Authentication. We do not store your password in plain text format.

1.2. Device Information

We collect device-specific information to ensure security and prevent unauthorized access:

  • Device ID: A unique identifier for your device, used to validate device authorization and prevent subscription sharing across multiple devices.

1.3. Authentication and Session Data

To maintain secure access to your account:

  • Session Token: A secure token generated for each login session to verify your identity and prevent unauthorized access.
  • Login Timestamps: Information about when you access the Application (last login time, session creation time).

1.4. Subscription and Usage Data

For subscription management and service delivery:

  • Subscription Status: Information about your subscription plan (active/inactive, expiry date, product ID).
  • Trial Usage: Count of trial prints used (for free tier users).
  • Subscription Verification Timestamps: Records of subscription status verification with Google Play.

1.5. Application Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our services, including:

  • Application features used
  • Error logs and crash reports (for debugging and improvement)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create, maintain, and manage your user account.
  • Authentication and Security: To verify your identity, prevent unauthorized access, and maintain account security through device validation.
  • Subscription Services: To manage your subscription, verify subscription status with Google Play, and provide access to premium features.
  • Communication: To send important account-related emails (e.g., email verification, password reset instructions).
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the Application's functionality, performance, and user experience.
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms and Conditions.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1. Cloud Storage

Your data is stored securely using the following services:

  • Firebase Authentication: Your email and encrypted password are stored securely by Google Firebase Authentication service.
  • Cloud Firestore: Your profile information, device ID, session data, and subscription information are stored in Google Cloud Firestore database.

3.2. Local Storage

Some data is stored locally on your device for app functionality:

  • Session tokens (encrypted in device storage)
  • Local subscription status (for offline functionality)
  • Application preferences and settings

3.3. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are hashed using secure encryption algorithms (Firebase Authentication).
  • Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.
  • Firebase Security Rules ensure only authorized users can access their own data.
  • Device ID validation prevents unauthorized access from other devices.

However, please be aware that:

  • No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
  • It is your responsibility to maintain the security of your device and account credentials.
  • We strongly advise against jailbreaking or rooting your device, as this may compromise security features.
